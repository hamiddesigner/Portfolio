SaaS Analytics Dashboard

Role Lead Product Designer
Timeline Jan 2024 - Mar 2024
Tools Figma, FigJam, Maze

I redesigned a complex B2B analytics platform used by 10,000+ users to track business metrics, reducing task completion time by 40% through improved information architecture and data visualization.

The Challenge

The existing analytics dashboard was overwhelming users with too much data displayed at once. Users struggled to find the metrics they needed, leading to decreased platform engagement and increased support tickets.

Key problems identified:

  • Information overload with 50+ metrics on a single screen
  • No customization or personalization options
  • Poor mobile responsiveness affecting 30% of users
  • Inconsistent design patterns across different modules

My Role & Responsibilities

As the lead product designer on this project, I was responsible for:

  • Conducting user research and competitive analysis
  • Creating information architecture and user flows
  • Designing high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes
  • Leading design reviews and stakeholder presentations
  • Collaborating with 3 engineers and 1 product manager
  • Conducting usability testing with 15+ users

Design Process & Iterations

1. Research & Discovery

I conducted 12 user interviews with existing customers to understand pain points and workflows. Key findings included:

  • Users only regularly check 8-10 metrics out of 50+
  • Most important task is comparing current vs. past performance
  • Users want to customize their dashboard layout

2. Information Architecture

I restructured the dashboard into modular widgets that users can customize. The new architecture groups related metrics and provides progressive disclosure.

3. Wireframes & Iterations

I created multiple wireframe iterations, testing different layouts with users. The final design emerged after 3 rounds of feedback.

4. Visual Design

I created a cohesive design system with consistent components, improved data visualization, and better use of color to highlight important metrics.

Final Solution

The redesigned dashboard features a modular, customizable interface that puts users in control of their data.

Key Features

Customizable Widget System

Users can add, remove, and rearrange widgets to create their ideal dashboard layout.

Smart Data Visualization

Improved charts and graphs make it easier to spot trends and compare metrics at a glance.

Responsive Design

Fully responsive layout works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
